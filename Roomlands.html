COMBAT

RULES:

  1. Draw the number of cards equal to player combat score.
  2. Draw enemy cards 1 at a time, counter if you have a higher number, Cancel ties, or use a lower number and take a WOUND.
  3. Counter with the same suit for a CRITICAL HIT (Remove one of the Enemies remaining attacks).
  4. You win the combat if you beat the majority of the incoming attacks.
  5. WINNING:
    • - Beating a ROOK - take Rook reward and add 1 to COMBAT or EXPLORATION score.
    • - Beating a HUMAN - take 1 TREASURE.
  6. WOUNDS - Count up wounds, for each wound, remove 1 point from COMBAT or EXPLORATION, if either reaches zero, you DIE!
Spades = Unarmed Attack | Clubs = Weapon Attack | Hearts = Magic Attack | Diamonds = Creative Attack
