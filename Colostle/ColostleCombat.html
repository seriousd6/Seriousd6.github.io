COMBAT

RULES:

  1. Draw the number of cards equal to player combat score. (People =1, Medium Rook =3, Massive =5, AR =6)
  2. Draw enemy cards 1 at a time, counter if you have a higher number, Cancel ties, or use a lower number and take a WOUND.
  3. Counter with the same suit for a CRITICAL HIT (Remove one of the Enemies remaining attacks).
  4. You win the combat if you beat the majority of the incoming attacks.
  5. WINNING:
    • - Beating a ROOK - take Rook reward and add 1 to COMBAT or EXPLORATION score.
    • - Beating a HUMAN - take 1 TREASURE.
  6. WOUNDS - Count up wounds, for each wound, remove 1 point from COMBAT or EXPLORATION, if either reaches zero, you DIE!
Spades = Unarmed Attack | Clubs = Weapon Attack | Hearts = Magic Attack | Diamonds = Creative Attack (AR) Spades = Laser Attack | Clubs = Flying Attack | Hearts = Grab Attack | Diamonds = Rocket Attack

Once all 52 cards are used, the website "deck" will be reshuffled automatically

LOSING Your opponent lives and you receive no reward. Any wounds received in this COMBAT phase still take effect - both on you and your opponent. Maybe you will encounter this opponent again - a ‘white whale’ for your character. If you do, this you can count any wounds you managed to land against it as still there and reduce its combat score accordingly.
