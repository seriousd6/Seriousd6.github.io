Study Guides

Structured multi-session guides for group or personal study. Each guide has teaching content, key passages, and discussion questions per session.

Hebrews: The Better Covenant

13 chapters · A chapter-by-chapter study tracking the sustained argument that Jesus is the fulfilment and surpassing of the old covenant.

Ephesians: Every Spiritual Blessing

6 sessions · From cosmic election to household ethics — Paul's most concentrated presentation of the gospel and its implications for the church.

Romans 1–8: The Righteousness of God

8 sessions · The universal problem of sin, justification by faith alone, union with Christ, and life in the Spirit.

The Sermon on the Mount

5 sessions · Matthew 5–7 — the Beatitudes, the Antitheses, prayer, anxiety, and the two ways — the ethics of the kingdom.

The Psalms: A 4-Week Devotional

4 sessions · Lament, praise, royal-messianic, and trust psalms — learning to pray the whole Psalter with Israel.
